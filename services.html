Comprehensive Healthcare Services

We provide a wide range of healthcare services and administrative support to serve our community better.

Medical Services

General Medicine

Comprehensive primary healthcare services for common illnesses and health maintenance.

  • Routine check-ups
  • Fever and infection treatment
  • Chronic disease management

Cardiology

Specialized cardiac care including diagnosis and treatment of heart conditions.

  • ECG and Echo tests
  • Blood pressure management
  • Cardiac rehabilitation

Orthopedics

Treatment of bone, joint, and muscle disorders with modern surgical techniques.

  • Fracture treatment
  • Joint replacement surgery
  • Sports injury treatment

Pediatrics

Specialized healthcare services for infants, children, and adolescents.

  • Child vaccination
  • Growth monitoring
  • Pediatric emergency care

Gynecology

Women's health services including pregnancy care and gynecological treatments.

  • Prenatal care
  • Normal and cesarean delivery
  • Family planning services

Emergency Services

24/7 emergency medical care with rapid response and life-saving treatments.

  • Trauma care
  • Ambulance services
  • Critical care unit

Administrative Services

Birth Certificate

Official birth certificate issuance for newborns delivered at our hospital.

Required Documents:

  • Hospital delivery record
  • Parents' identification
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)

Processing Time: 2-3 working days

Death Certificate

Official death certificate for patients who passed away at our hospital.

Required Documents:

  • Hospital death record
  • Family member identification
  • Relationship proof

Processing Time: 1-2 working days

Swasthya Sathi Registration

Registration for West Bengal government's health insurance scheme for free medical treatment.

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar card
  • Ration card
  • Bank account details
  • Income certificate

Processing Time: 7-10 working days
