Comprehensive Medical Facilities

Our hospital is equipped with modern medical facilities and advanced equipment to provide comprehensive healthcare services to our patients.

Diagnostic Services

Digital X-Ray

High-resolution digital X-ray services for accurate bone and chest imaging with minimal radiation exposure.

  • Digital radiography system
  • Instant image processing
  • Low radiation dose

Ultrasound

Advanced ultrasound imaging for pregnancy monitoring, abdominal scans, and cardiac evaluations.

  • 4D ultrasound capability
  • Doppler studies
  • Real-time imaging

CT Scan

Multi-slice CT scanner for detailed cross-sectional imaging of the body for accurate diagnosis.

  • 16-slice CT scanner
  • Contrast studies available
  • Emergency CT services

MRI Scan

Magnetic Resonance Imaging for detailed soft tissue visualization without radiation exposure.

  • 1.5 Tesla MRI system
  • Brain and spine imaging
  • Joint and soft tissue scans
